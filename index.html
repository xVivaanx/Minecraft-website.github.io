Introduction

Minecraft was offcially released on 18 November 2011 by the founder Markus Persson a Swedish game engineer.
Minecraft was made on the programming language Java. Shortly after the release Jens Bergensten (commonly know as Jeb) was given the position of Development.
Minecraft has since been ported to several other platforms and it is the Best selling game of All Time , with over 200 million copies sold and 130 million monthly active users!
Markus Persson sold Minecraft to Mojang for 2.5 Billion dollars, later Mojang discontinued Markus Personn as he deleted all his social media accounts.

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a video game in which players create and break apart various kinds of blocks in three-dimensional worlds.
The gameâ€™s two main modes are Survival and Creative. In Survival, players must find their own building supplies and food.
They also interact with blocklike mobs, or moving creatures. (Creepers and zombies are some of the dangerous ones.)
In Creative, players are given supplies and do not have to eat to survive. They also can break all kinds of blocks immediately.
The purpose of the game is simply to build and explore (and survive)
You can play by yourself or you can play online with others. The smartphone and tablet versions offer multi-player options through WiFi networks.
Players can connect to thousands of Minecraft online games (servers), some of which involve battling other players.
